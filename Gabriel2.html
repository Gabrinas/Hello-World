The Three Musketeers and D'artagnan

Athos, Parthos, with Aramis made up the three Musketeers. They are had a boy by name, D'artagnan who later joined the three able body guard. They had a slogan: All for One One For All, which is mostly said by them and they are known for it.

The description of the three musketeers

Athos

A man full of skill in sword fights. He was once a lover to Milady. He is one of the three musketeers.

Porthos

A man of body physique amd well built. He is tall and full of confidence during fight. He could use his head to knock down an enemies.

Aramis

The third of the three musketeers, he always put on a priest like chain depicting his religious likeness. He is tall as well.
